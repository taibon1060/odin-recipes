Summertime Mango Drink

A cool and fresh drink for the summer. You can double the ingredients for 6 to 8 people.

Prep: 5 mins
Total: 5 mins
Servings: 4
Yield: 4 servings

Ingredients

  • 2 mangoes, cut into cubes
  • 1 cup ice cubes
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 ⅔ cups milk

Steps

  1. Combine mangoes, ice cubes, vanilla extract, and sugar in a blender; pour in milk. Blend until liquefied.
